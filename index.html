What to Wear

Due to the harsh conditions, freezing temperatures, and lack of Oxygen, everyone is required to wear government-certified space suits. Each suit is equipped with state-of-the-art technology such as temperature control, communication with up to 5 miles of range and an oxygen tank that will last the average person around 5 hours.
